1.What kind of words did you find? 
They are the words with high log-odds ratio, which means that they appears frequently in one data set, while seldom appears in the other.
And their log ratio is quite similar, and almost in the same level.

2.Are there any overlapping words between the two lists? 
There is no overlapping.

3.How are these words different from what you found with topwords.py / topwords.java?
Words in topwords.py are those frequently appears in almost every article, and words rank high with log-odds ratio indicate the difference of
articles. Words in the two lists could be considered as the features of one kind of corpus.
